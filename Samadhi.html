Samadhi

Samadhi :-

Samadhi means equal feeling. By getting this stage the mind comes to a higher level where there is no dual feeling. This is the eighth part of Ashtanga yoga. After getting concentration and discipline of mind we get control over each chakra. And by Samadhi one can abstain from life for a time being or forever. In Savikalpasamadhi the practitioner can return from Samadhi to life after a few days. When he feels that his work in this world is finished he can take Nirvikalpasamadhi which is equal to death.
