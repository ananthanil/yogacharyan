Abdomen Exercises

Agnisarakriya

Breathing out, contract and release the abdomen muscles several times. relax a little and then repeat.

Evaccuation

Breath out fully by bending the body forward and press out the air from the stomach also. Come up without breathing in and hold back the abdomen muscles for few seconds. Release the abdomen muscles and then breath in. Repeat three or four times. (Uddian Bendha)
