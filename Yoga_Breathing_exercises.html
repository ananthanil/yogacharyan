Breathing Exercises

(1) Kapalabhathi

Sitting erect, hold back the abdomen muscles when breathing out forcefully at a stretch (not slowly). Breathing in quickly release the abdomen muscles. Repeat fifteen to twenty times and then relax a few seconds and do it again. This is a cleaning process for the respiratory system.

(2) Anuloma viloma pranayama

Use the thumb and ring finger of right hand to close the nostrils whenever it is necessary. Breath out through the left nostril slowly (by 8 sec) and breath in through the same by somewhat half of it (4 sec). And hold it for a few seconds.(4 sec) Then breath out through the right and breath in through the same. Repeat twenty times through each nostril.
