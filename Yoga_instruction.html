Instructions

Yoga is not a mere exercise. It is a life style. Just like brushing the teeth daily, yoga also must be a part of our life. Then only it is beneficial. Yoga can be practiced early in the morning or in the evening or both time. If after a meal wait for 2:30 hours. Empty the bowels and bladder if needed, wear minimum cotton cloths, use mat or sheet and do the exercises either in a well ventilated room or open space. Most of the exercises are connected with breathing and must be done synchronized with breathing very slowly. Each item can be repeated according to the time schedule allotted for exercises; but never get exhausted by exercise. For saving time it is advisable to repeat each item three or five times if it is a movement, and for still items take three or five breathing for each. If after a major operation, yoga can be started after one year. When feeling ill, should not practice yoga and take rest. Ladies must avoid yoga during menstrual period and after three months of pregnancy. They can start yoga three months after delivery. During pregnancy they can have some selected items of yoga prescribed by a master which will help them for a natural delivery. Better results can be attained by taking vegetarian diet and avoiding tobacco in any form and alcohol. Do not attempt difficult items by giving strain to the body. Do any item only up to the level of the flexibility of the body, The body will attain more flexibility and then difficult postures also can be practiced later.

Inversion postures like head standing and shoulder standing must not be attempted by persons having high blood pressure and heart complaints.

If it is for maintenance of good health to lead a happy and contented life practice of difficult yoga postures is not needed Warming up the body with some easy preliminary exercises a few postures of yoga by standing, sitting and lying and some eye exercises, breathing exercises (Pranayama) and abdomen exercises can be practiced by 20 or 25 minutes. This website is not designed as a guide for beginners. They must get the help of a master because the website is not capable of making any corrections when the beginner is going wrong. More over the practitioner will be always doubtful regarding the correctness of his postures. When a master gives confidence by commenting, the beginner will have a psychological effect. Unlike other methods, yoga is giving exercises for all parts of body. This needs no equipments. Therefor this can be practiced anywhere in the world if we get six feet space.
