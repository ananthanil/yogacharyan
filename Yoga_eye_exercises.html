Eye Exercises

(1) Exercises of eyes

Exercises of eyes are very simple and easy to do and may feel very silly. But the effect of it is very important as it helps us to get sharp eye sight in any age.

1, Squeezing and releasing several times.

2, Blinking for ten sends.

1, Moving eye balls up and down keeping at the extremities one second each.

2, Side to side.

3, Diagonally.

4, Rotation in both directions.

5, Bringing both to the centre to look at the tip of the nose.

6, Look at a distant object for three seconds, then close the eyes for three seconds then open the eyes to a near object. The lines on the palm can be the near object, Repeat three or five times.
