Human Body

Panchabhootha and Panchakosha:-

Human body is made of five primary elements namely earth, water, fire, air and ether. There are five kosas- annamaya the body, pranamaya the life, manomaya the mind, vijnanamaya the knowledge and anandamaya the bliss. By taking food and water we develop annamaya kosa. By pranayama (controlled breathing) pranamaya kosa is developing. Dhyana (meditation) improves manomaya kosa. Progress in mediation leads to formation of sixth sense- intuition by which we can get the maximum possible knowledge in this world. Thus vijnanamaya kosa is developed. When we feel that there is nothing more to understand in this world, we enjoy the ultimate pleasure and thus the anandamaya kosa is also opened.

Yoga is union

There are three energies working in us. Jeevatma is the life (bio magnetic energy), Chitha the mind (conscious energy) and Paramatma the subconscious mind (cosmic energy). Yoga the word means uniting together. Here Jeevatma is united with Paramatma. This union is possible only after de- functioning mind, which is working as a security in between Jeevatma and Paramatma. Pathanjaly the great sage who designed ashtanga yoga gives this as "yogaschithavrithinirodha". When we get access to the sub conscious level of mind actually the individual soul is getting connected with the supreme soul and this will be helping us to unveil the secrets of this universe. This reveals the past, present and future of our own life. This is termed as self realization which is the ultimate aim of yoga.

Disease and Treatment.

"Body is the greatest doctor, food is the greatest medicine. Disease is the necessity of the body. Never suppressit. Allow it to run its course"

Human body is made of five primary elements viz. earth, water, air, fire and ether. We will never attempt to repair wooden furniture with cement or iron. Just like that human body must be refreshed by the five elements mentioned above. In this case earth we utilize as food. Water is used for drinking and baths, air for breathing and fire from sun light. But we are not thinking about ether. If we are living with nature, the nature will be providing the facilities for refreshing the body with all the five elements and we will be healthy. This is why the animals in the forest are not having any veterinary doctor to treat them. As we are living against nature in many aspects, due to the imbalance of our life style, unwanted things are accumulating in our body. The body will be tolerating this for a long time and is waiting for a convenient time to react against it. The reaction will be in the form of a fever or head ache. This is a sewage work. For the digestion of a full belly food, the body is utilizing about 90% of its energy. Hence the body cannot undertake any sewage work when we take three times meals without break. There for the important symptom of a fever is not having appetite and taste of food. If you accept the demand of the body and take no food, the stomach becomes empty and this time ether is used for refreshing the body. We cannot undertake the work of cooking when we clean the kitchen.

If we allow the fever to run its course by taking rest and not eating, the body will be rectifying all the defects and help us to restore good health, But we have no time to wait for seven days for this. We go to a doctor for the treatment. Doctor prescribes some medicines which must be taken after food. When food enters the stomach, the body cannot go on with the sewage work and then the symptoms of the fever disappear and the body is turning to digestion work. Thus you suppress the fever not satisfying the necessity of the body. The body will be waiting for another chance and the rectification will be appearing as another disease. Thus the medicine was not curing the disease but was changing it as another disease.

Fasting

Instead of waiting for the body to start its rectification process with a fever, we can help the body in this matter by providing the circumstances for this by taking a fasting for one day fortnightly. On fasting day we must drink more water, especially at the times of meals. In meal time the digestive juices injected to the stomach by the body must be diluted. Otherwise they will be corroding the walls of the stomach resulting to gastric ulcer. Moreover the first meal taking for breaking the fast on the next day must not be heavy. The old people of India were selecting the eleventh day after the full moon and new moon for fasting as they understood that the eleventh day is connected with digestion. As this need not come on a holiday, the employed people can have a convenient holiday for this.

Good Habits of Eating

Keeping healthy habits of eating will help us in a large extent to minimize the imbalance of food. Drink your solids and eat your liquids. Solids must be chewed well to mix it with saliva and then you can drink it. Liquids must be kept in the mouth for a few seconds to mix it with saliva and then gulp it. By chewing the solids the digestive juice gets more area for attack. There is no chewing mechanism (teeth) below the mouth, Number of times of meals a day also must be limited to two times with breakfast and lunch. During night time the digestion is very low. The day beings stop eating by twilight. If you are so particular to take dinner, take it before sunset and it must be very light. It is better to take one time meal without cooking because some of the very important things like insulin evaporate when heated above 100 degree Celsius. If you take cooked and uncooked together, take the uncooked first and the cooked must be following it. If you take water just before and along with the meals, it will dilute the digestive juice and it will not be strong enough to attack the food material successfully. Take water either one hour before or one hour after the meals.

Nose is placed near the mouth to understand the smell of the food. Cow understands the eatable grass not by seeing it, but by smelling it. Think of the nasty smell of a fish market from where you buy your food. Actually it is not good smelling. By adding spices you change its flavor and then eat it. If you see a dead ant inside a cup of tea, you will not use it due to presence of dead body in it. It is fantastic that you accept a large dead body of a fish as your food material. The shape and arrangement of human teeth are also not apt for meat eating. The vegetarian meals that we take will be digested by 24 hours and the waste will be thrown out after that. But the non vegetarian meals digest by 18 hours and the waste will be kept inside the abdomen for 6 more hours. During this time the body will be absorbing many unwanted things from it causing accumulation of more fat resulting to overweight. Use of alcohol deteriorates the efficiency of nervous system. This is dangerous than death.
